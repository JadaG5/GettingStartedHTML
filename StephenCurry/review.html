About Stephen Curry

Wardell Stephen Curry II (born March 14, 1988), better known as Stephen Curry, is a professional American basketball player with the Golden State Warriors.

Steph's Net worth, Salary, and Contract

As of 2019, Stephen Curry has a reported net worth of $90 million. In 2012, Curry signed a four-year contract extension with the Golden State Warriors. Curry’s $12.1 yearly salary made him just the 85th-highest paid NBA player in the 2016-17 season, despite his two-time MVP status. However, in June 2017, Curry made up for his previous bargain rate and then some by signing the NBA’s highest-paid contract ever: a $201 million salary over five years that will take him through the 2020-21 season.
