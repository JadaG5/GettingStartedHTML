About Peezy

Peezy started his career as a street rapper in Detroit at a very young age. Rapping became the go-to voice for him that would help in releasing the stress caused by his childhood struggles. Slowly down the line, Peezy started releasing albums like Mud In Mud Out ,Number 1 Ghetto Boy, Mud, Money Orientation, Sweat & Tears, and many others which became a massive hit.
